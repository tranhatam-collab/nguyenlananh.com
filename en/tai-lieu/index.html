1 · Introduction

Nguyenlananh.com is a content and companionship platform for going inward, untangling the loops that run your life, and rebuilding your life from awareness to real action. This is not a blog. It is a practice system with a method, a roadmap, check-ins, assessment, and measurable outputs.

Who it's for

  • People who feel stuck repeating the same loop — in work, relationships, habits, or emotions — and can't break free.
  • People who have read a lot, understood a lot, but haven't turned awareness into real, consistent, measurable action.
  • People ready to look at themselves honestly — no avoidance, no cosmetic fixes, no blame-shifting.

Who it's not for

  • People seeking a quick fix, a single coaching session that changes their life. That doesn't exist here.
  • People in acute mental health crisis (severe depression, clinical anxiety, suicidal ideation). This system is not therapy — you need a medical professional.
  • People who want to be diagnosed, told "you have this, take this." No diagnosis, no prescriptions, no therapy here.
Honesty: This system helps you see the real problem and take small real action. It does not replace psychotherapy, medical care, or legal/financial advice. If you are in acute crisis, contact a medical professional or a mental health hotline in your country.

2 · Method

The system runs on four steps and four axes. Every article, product, and program fits within this frame — nothing exists outside it.

Four steps

1 · Observe

Look at your mind, reactions, living environment, and relationship with objects each day. No judgment — just seeing.

2 · Feel

Touch objects, feel the space, sense your body-mind state to know how you're actually living.

3 · Act

Clean, sweep, do manual work, arrange your environment. Small, consistent, measurable actions.

4 · Transform

From stuck to a new flow, from old reflex to conscious choice and creating new value.

Four content axes

  • Inner — loops, awareness, emotions, silence, truth with self.
  • Material — cleaning, space, living environment, objects and behavior.
  • Family system — roles, unspoken rules, intergenerational patterns, money, conflict.
  • Action & labor — creative work, self-investment, life rhythm, capacity to produce output.

7 · FAQ

Is Nguyenlananh.com psychotherapy?

No. This is a life-practice system with a method. No diagnosis, no prescriptions, no therapy. If you're in acute mental health crisis, contact a medical professional or a hotline.

Do I have to pay from the start?

No. Steps 1 and 2 are free (read articles + free member). You only pay when you choose to go deeper — and only after you're in the right system, understanding what you're buying.

What is a certification worth?

Certification acknowledges you completed a program, submitted evidence, passed the final exam, and met the rubric. It's evidence of capacity within the Nguyenlananh.com system — not a national degree, not a professional license. Its value depends on who accepts it and the context of use.

How long until I see results?

Identification: 7–15 days. Real practice: 21–90 days. Specialization: 6–12 months. There's no "one session changes your life." This system measures by real action, not temporary emotion.

Can I do it without buying anything?

Yes. All public articles (76) are free. You can practice the four-step method on your own without buying anything. Paid products are for people who need structure, assessment, evidence, and companionship — not "secret content."

Why are products #3 (Emotional Block) and #5 (Family Pattern) on HOLD?

Because they touch sensitive territory (heavy emotions, family systems). Not available until ethics policy, escalation protocol (when to refer to medical professional), and legal disclaimer are complete. Participant safety over revenue.

I'm outside Vietnam, how do I buy?

Currently Vietnam only (VietQR). PayPal/Stripe on HOLD, waiting for Gate 1/2. When it opens, this page will be updated.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. See Privacy Policy. Evidence data (practice, rubric, reports) is encrypted, not shared with third parties, never sold.

Can I stop midway?

Yes. You can stop at any time. No time-binding contract. Refunds per the policy in section 6.

Does Nguyễn Lan Anh personally companion me?

At the high tier (certification, programs with review) yes. At the low tier (self-assessment, micro) no — you follow guidance yourself. "Reviewed" tiers in the price table clearly indicate a real person is involved.

If I'm not a fit, will someone tell me?

Yes. Each product's entry test includes screening. If results show you're better suited for medical/therapeutic care, the system will recommend you seek a professional — it won't keep selling to you.

Can I become a companion/guide?

Yes, through the T4 path: Certified Practice Companion L1 → Practice Method Designer. This is a specialization path with exams, defense, and certification. Not "sign up and you're in."

Is this page updated?

Yes. Every time a product is added, price changes, status changes, this page updates. Version is noted at the top. This is the single source of truth for pricing & products — if you hear a price elsewhere, verify here.
