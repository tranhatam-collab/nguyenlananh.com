You Are Not Less Capable, You Are in the Wrong Place

Sometimes the problem is not that you are not good enough.

A tree that doesn’t grow is not weak. The soil may be wrong.

The same applies to people.

You try harder. Learn more. Adjust your approach.

Yet nothing moves.

The environment may not support your way of growing.

A right environment expands you. A wrong one makes you doubt yourself.

Sometimes the change is not within you. It is where you stand.

You are not wrong. Your placement is.
