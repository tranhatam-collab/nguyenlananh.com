## 1. Sales Principle

NguyenLanAnh.com should not sell by pressure. It sells by helping a person choose correctly, including the choice not to buy yet.

Core sales line:

```text
Nếu chỉ cần một câu hỏi, hãy đọc bài public. Nếu cần một nhịp, vào Core. Nếu một mô thức đã lặp lại có bằng chứng, vào Deep. Nếu một mảng đời sống cần thiết kế lại trong 30, 60 hoặc 90 ngày, hãy cân nhắc Pro.
```

Never use:

- fear of missing out
- shame-based urgency
- promises of becoming a new person
- public commission language
- inflated before/after claims
- package copy that hides what is not included

### Checkout 01 — Gói này mở những gì

Core Access opens the seven-day foundation, 30-day practice, 10 pillar companion lessons, core worksheets, and chooser.

Deep Continuity opens everything in Core plus 12 deep tracks, deep worksheets, case/practice library, and monthly review prompts.

Mastery / Pro opens everything in Core and Deep plus eight Pro tracks, implementation workbooks, 30/60/90-day plans, and creator eligibility review.

This system does not promise a new life overnight. It does not promise another person will change. It does not replace therapy, medical care, legal advice, financial advice, or crisis support. It does not make practice unnecessary.

It gives structure, language, worksheets, routes, and rhythm. The member still needs to bring the work into real days.

### Checkout 03 — Bạn cần chuẩn bị thời gian thật nào

Core: 10 minutes a day.  
Deep: 15-20 minutes a day for one chosen track.  
Pro: 30-60 minutes several times per week, depending on track.

1. Go to `/members/dashboard/`.

## 5. Creator / Collaborator Curriculum

Creator access is an internal quality path. It is not automatic after payment and not a public affiliate program.

### Creator 01 — Voice lock của NguyenLanAnh

Objective:

The collaborator learns to recognize and protect the voice: grounded, quiet, specific, not dramatic.

Training content:

NguyenLanAnh voice does not try to sound wise. It looks at ordinary life until something true becomes visible. It does not rush to explain. It does not decorate pain. It does not turn every paragraph into advice.

Exercise:

- Rewrite a generic motivational paragraph into a grounded paragraph.
- Remove big claims.
- Add one real-life object or moment.

Review criteria:

- no hype
- no moralizing
- no abstract inspiration without practice
- one grounded image

### Creator 02 — Viết không giảng, không chữa, không hứa

Objective:

The collaborator learns boundaries of tone and scope.

Training content:

Do not write as if the reader is a student being corrected. Do not write as if the content can heal a person. Do not promise outcomes. Write as if the reader is an adult who can look at their life with support and space.

Exercise:

- Take one paragraph with lecture tone.
- Remove commands that shame.
- Replace promises with practice-based language.

Review criteria:

- no diagnosis
- no forced solution
- no promise
- clear practice

### Creator 03 — Biến một public pillar thành paid practice

Objective:

The collaborator turns recognition into practice.

Training content:

Public pillar: opens a truth.  
Paid lesson: gives one way to work with that truth this week.  
Worksheet: captures evidence.  
Chooser: tells the member where to go next.

Exercise:

- Choose one public pillar.
- Write one paid lesson intro.
- Add one practice and three prompts.

Review criteria:

- maps to one pillar
- practice under 20 minutes
- prompts are answerable
- CTA matches tier

### Creator 04 — Case thật không xâm phạm riêng tư

Objective:

The collaborator learns to use real-life cases without exposing people.

Training content:

A case should preserve pattern, not identity. Remove location, job, family role specifics, timing, rare details, quotes that can identify someone, and combinations of facts that reveal a person.

Exercise:

- Rewrite a detailed case into a pattern case.
- Keep the pattern.
- Remove identity signals.

Review criteria:

- no identifiable detail
- no private confession used as content
- no blaming a person
- pattern remains useful

### Creator 05 — Internal links và route ổn định

Objective:

## 6. Admin / CMS Process

### Content Inventory

Admin must show these inventory groups:

- Public Pillars
- Core Member Lessons
- Deep Tracks
- Pro Tracks
- Creator Curriculum
- Worksheets
- Offers
- Entitlements
- Creator Submissions
- Release Checklist

### Content Workflow

```text
draft -> in_review -> approved -> published -> archived
```

Status definitions:

- `draft`: work exists but is not ready for review.
- `in_review`: editor/operator is checking voice, route, tier, and completeness.
- `approved`: owner/lead has approved content for release.
- `published`: content is live or generated into site output.
- `archived`: content is retired but kept for audit.

### Admin Release Checklist

Before publish:

- VI copy checked.
- EN copy checked or EN brief approved.
- Voice checked against canon.
- Stable links only.
- Planned routes are not exposed as live public links.
- Tier required is explicit.
- Offer/plan mapping is explicit.
- Worksheet exists if lesson requires it.
- Caution note exists for family, children, money, health-adjacent, or emotional topics.
- Route smoke passes.
- Report updated with timestamp.

### Offer / Entitlement Procedure

When a paid offer is added or changed:

1. Confirm plan code: `year1`, `year2`, or `year3`.
2. Confirm included routes.
3. Confirm excluded routes.
4. Confirm checkout copy says what is not promised.
5. Confirm entitlement code and route pattern.
6. Confirm D1/admin changes are reviewed before deploy.
7. Run payment health smoke.
8. Update report.

### Creator Submission Procedure

1. Creator submits full packet.
2. Editor checks voice and practice.
3. Operator checks route, links, image brief, and tier.
4. Owner/lead approves or requests revision.
5. Admin publishes only after approval.
6. Audit log stores actor, action, entity, before/after, reason.

Revision note template:

```text
Decision: revision required
Reason:
Voice:
Practice:
Route/tier:
Required change:
Review again after:
```

Approval note template:

```text
Decision: approved
Approved by:
Route:
Tier:
Mapped pillar/program:
Publish window:
Report updated:
```

### Support Procedure For Access Questions

When a member reports missing access:

1. Confirm account email.
2. Confirm payment order or free member session.
3. Confirm plan code and entitlement.
4. Check whether route requires higher tier.
5. If entitlement mismatch exists, escalate to admin runtime flow.
6. Do not manually promise access without order evidence.
7. Log action in report/admin audit.

### Homepage Refresh Procedure

Homepage remains blocked until:

- Core, Deep, Pro and Creator content are visible or properly gated.
- Entitlement map is implemented or documented in runtime.
- Admin checklist exists.
- Production route smoke passes.
- Owner approves final messaging.

Homepage copy must present:

- public writings
- free join
- Core as first paid rhythm
- Deep as structured practice
- Pro as implementation
- Creator as internal quality path

Homepage copy must not present:

- public commission promise
- exaggerated transformation claims
- unbuilt route links
- pressure-buy CTA
