Space Rebuild — 30 days

Change your space to change your rhythm — durably, in 15–30 minutes a day, without decluttering the whole house and burning out.

Register free to begin Read the base article

This is for you if

  • It's messy again days after you tidy up
  • Your space tires you and you're not sure why
  • You want a maintenance system, not a one-time overhaul

What you receive

  • Room-by-room rebuild map + 7-layer checklist (printable PDF)
  • A three-question keep/release card for each item
  • A 30-day calendar, one 15–30 minute cell per day
  • A 10-minute nightly rhythm and the one-in-one-out rule

The 30-day path

  • Week 1 · Surface & flow. Each day free up one flat surface.
  • Week 2 · Volume. Each day bring one category to the amount you truly use.
  • Week 3 · Zoning, light, air. Assign each area one function, add light and airflow.
  • Week 4 · Meaning & maintenance. Handle hard-to-release items and set a holding rhythm.

How to take part

  1. Register free with email and receive a magic link.
  2. Complete a basic profile so the system places you at the right starting point.
  3. Unlock the rebuild map and the maintenance track in the members layer.
