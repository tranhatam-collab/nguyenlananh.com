What Path Am I Really On?

A reflective checkpoint for people who are moving fast but unsure whether their path still feels true.

Main Insight

Many people are moving, but not all movement is direction.

A true path is not just efficient. It is aligned with your values, your energy, and the life you actually want to build.

Asking this question early can save years of silent misalignment.

Practice Step

Write down your current path in one sentence, then rewrite it in a way that feels honest and sustainable.
