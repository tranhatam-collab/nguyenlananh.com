The Cleaning Practice: A Foundational View

An overview of cleaning as a life practice connecting inner awareness, environment, and behavior.

Main Insight

The cleaning practice links inner life and outer life in a direct way.

Your space reflects your inner state, and your inner state is shaped by your space. This relationship is practical, not symbolic only.

By cleaning consciously, you train attention, restore order, and create conditions for creativity and healing.

Practice Step

Walk through your space and remove five items that no longer support the life you want to live.
