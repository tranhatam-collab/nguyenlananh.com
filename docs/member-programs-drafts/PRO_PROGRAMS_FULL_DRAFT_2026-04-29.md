Status: DRAFT FOR OWNER REVIEW — not committed, not deployed

Pro is not a bigger archive. Pro is where a member takes one life system seriously for 30, 60, or 90 days. Each track must be specific enough to guide action and honest enough to say who should not enter yet.

---

## 1. Pro Program Standard

Each Pro program must include:

- who this is for
- who should wait
- what it unlocks
- what it does not promise
- eight modules
- workbook fields
- implementation calendar
- completion criteria
- next step chooser

Pro entry rule:

```text
A member should enter Pro only after completing Core and at least one Deep track with evidence from practice.
```

Pro tone:

- practical
- direct
- no hype
- no guru posture
- no "new identity" promise
- no public revenue or commission language

## Pro 01 — Reset Life

Route: `/members/pro/reset/`  
Tier: `year3`  
Duration: 30 days

Who this is for:

- The member feels several parts of life are tangled at once.
- They cannot tell whether the main issue is work, relationship, body, money, environment, or rhythm.
- They need a starting structure before choosing a specialized track.

Who should wait:

- The member is looking for a dramatic restart.
- The member has not completed a seven-day Core practice.
- The member is in an urgent safety, legal, medical, or crisis situation that needs direct practical support first.

Program promise:

Reset Life helps the member rebuild a minimum viable life rhythm. It does not erase the past or solve every domain.

Modules:

1. `Ai cần Reset, ai chưa cần`  
   The member distinguishes real readiness from emotional urgency. They name the signs that life is too scattered for one isolated practice.  
   Output: Reset readiness note.

2. `Bản đồ đời sống hiện tại`  
   The member maps five areas: body, room, work, relationship, money/time. The goal is to see the actual surface, not make a life vision board.  
   Output: current life map.

3. `Một vòng lặp chính`  
   The member selects one central loop that appears across multiple areas.  
   Output: central loop map.

4. `Nhịp ngày tối thiểu`  
   The member designs a day with only three anchors: wake, work block, close-down.  
   Output: minimum day rhythm.

5. `Môi trường chống trượt`  
   The member sets up one physical and one digital environment change to reduce relapse into old rhythm.  
   Output: anti-slip environment setup.

6. `Khi quay lại lối cũ`  
   The member writes a return protocol for days that collapse.  
   Output: return script.

7. `Kế hoạch 30 ngày`  
   The member builds a four-week plan: stabilize, reduce, repeat, review.  
   Output: 30-day calendar.

8. `Chọn track tiếp theo`  
   The member chooses whether to continue Core, enter Inner, Discipline, Environment, Wealth, Family, Children, or Creation.  
   Output: next step decision.

Workbook fields:

- current life map
- central loop
- minimum day rhythm
- physical environment adjustment
- digital environment adjustment
- return protocol
- 30-day calendar
- completion review

30-day calendar:

- Week 1: stabilize wake, meal, and work/rest anchor.
- Week 2: reduce one source of noise or overcommitment.
- Week 3: repeat one small action daily.
- Week 4: review evidence and choose next track.

Completion criteria:

- 20 of 30 days tracked
- one central loop named
- one environment adjustment held for 14 days
- return protocol used at least once
- next track chosen with evidence

## Pro 02 — Inner

Route: `/members/pro/inner/`  
Tier: `year3`  
Duration: 30 days

Who this is for:

- The member has repeated emotional reactions they can now observe.
- Core and Deep have shown the same belief, fear, or body reaction more than once.
- The member can practice gently without turning inner work into self-attack.

Who should wait:

- The member wants to force emotional release.
- The member is in active crisis that requires direct qualified support.
- The member cannot yet keep a basic daily grounding rhythm.

Program promise:

Inner helps the member relate differently to emotional reflexes and old beliefs. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace qualified care.

Modules:

1. `Phản ứng gốc`  
   The member chooses one recurring reaction: freeze, control, please, withdraw, attack, over-explain.  
   Output: root reaction profile.

2. `Cảm xúc không được có chỗ`  
   The member names the feeling that usually has no room: anger, grief, fear, shame, need, softness.  
   Output: blocked feeling note.

3. `Niềm tin đang vận hành`  
   The member identifies the sentence underneath the reaction.  
   Output: belief statement.

4. `Cơ thể khi bị chạm`  
   The member tracks body signals when the pattern is activated.  
   Output: body map.

5. `Tự nói thật không tự công kích`  
   The member practices truthful internal language without punishment.  
   Output: inner sentence set.

6. `Ngày lặp lại`  
   The member prepares for a day when the old reaction returns.  
   Output: recurrence protocol.

7. `Kế hoạch 30 ngày`  
   The member sets a daily three-minute check-in and one weekly review.  
   Output: 30-day inner rhythm.

8. `Tích hợp vào quan hệ`  
   The member chooses one relationship where a small external change can reflect the internal work.  
   Output: relational integration step.

Workbook fields:

- root reaction
- blocked feeling
- belief statement
- body map
- honest inner sentence
- recurrence protocol
- relationship integration

30-day calendar:

- Week 1: observe reaction and body signal.
- Week 2: name feeling and belief.
- Week 3: practice internal language.
- Week 4: apply one small relational change.

Completion criteria:

- one reaction profile complete
- body signals tracked at least 10 times
- one belief statement named
- one recurrence protocol used
- one relationship integration step attempted

## Pro 03 — Discipline

Route: `/members/pro/discipline/`  
Tier: `year3`  
Duration: 30 days

Who this is for:

- The member understands what to do but cannot keep doing it.
- The issue is rhythm, not lack of knowledge.
- The member needs humane structure, not harsher self-talk.

Who should wait:

- The member wants discipline to punish themselves into worth.
- The member has not simplified the action enough.
- The member is trying to fix ten habits at once.

Program promise:

Discipline helps the member build a repeatable rhythm with room for missed days and returns.

Modules:

1. `Kỷ luật không phải tự ép`  
   The member removes shame language from discipline.  
   Output: humane discipline definition.

2. `Việc nhỏ cố định`  
   The member selects one fixed action with a minimum version.  
   Output: one-action contract.

3. `Lịch tuần thật`  
   The member maps the real week, not the ideal week.  
   Output: weekly rhythm map.

4. `Hệ thống nhắc nhẹ`  
   The member builds cues, friction reduction, and visible reminders.  
   Output: cue system.

5. `Ngày thất bại`  
   The member writes a missed-day plan before missing happens.  
   Output: missed-day protocol.

6. `Tăng độ khó không tăng cái tôi`  
   The member learns when to increase action without turning growth into ego performance.  
   Output: difficulty rule.

7. `Kế hoạch 30 ngày`  
   The member tracks action through minimum, standard, and rest versions.  
   Output: 30-day tracker.

8. `Duy trì sau khi hết mới`  
   The member designs maintenance after novelty fades.  
   Output: maintenance plan.

Workbook fields:

- one-action contract
- minimum version
- weekly rhythm
- cue system
- missed-day protocol
- difficulty rule
- maintenance plan

30-day calendar:

- Week 1: minimum version only.
- Week 2: standard version on three chosen days.
- Week 3: add cue and environment support.
- Week 4: test missed-day return and maintenance.

Completion criteria:

- one action repeated at least 20 days
- missed-day protocol used without self-attack
- no more than one main habit added
- maintenance plan written

## Pro 04 — Environment

Route: `/members/pro/environment/`  
Tier: `year3`  
Duration: 60 days

Who this is for:

- The member sees that environment repeatedly overrides intention.
- Physical space, digital input, schedule, and relational climate need redesign.
- The member can change some conditions without trying to control everything.

Who should wait:

- The member wants to blame every choice on environment.
- The member wants a visual makeover instead of behavior support.
- The member is not ready to remove inputs that keep the old rhythm alive.

Program promise:

Environment helps the member redesign conditions so the right action becomes easier and the old pull becomes weaker.

Modules:

1. `Môi trường hiện tại`  
   Audit rooms, phone, calendar, work surface, social climate.  
   Output: environment inventory.

2. `Nguồn vào số`  
   Identify feeds, chats, notifications, background content, and comparison loops.  
   Output: digital input audit.

3. `Không gian vật lý`  
   Design one space for one behavior.  
   Output: physical space setup.

4. `Người và lịch`  
   Notice how repeated interactions and schedule shape energy.  
   Output: relational and calendar map.

5. `Thiết kế vi môi trường`  
   Build small cues and remove friction.  
   Output: three micro-environments.

6. `Khi môi trường cũ kéo lại`  
   Prepare for travel, family visits, busy weeks, and old rooms.  
   Output: old-environment protocol.

7. `Kế hoạch 60 ngày`  
   Implement changes in two 30-day waves.  
   Output: 60-day map.

8. `Review mùa sống`  
   Review what should stay, change, or be released.  
   Output: seasonal review.

Workbook fields:

- environment inventory
- digital input audit
- one-space-one-behavior plan
- relational climate notes
- calendar pressure points
- micro-environment designs
- old-environment protocol
- 60-day map

60-day calendar:

- Days 1-10: audit without changing everything.
- Days 11-30: change one physical and one digital environment.
- Days 31-45: build three micro-environments.
- Days 46-60: review and stabilize.

Completion criteria:

- one digital input removed or reduced for 30 days
- one physical setup held for 30 days
- three micro-environments tested
- seasonal review complete

## Pro 05 — Creation

Route: `/members/pro/creation/`  
Tier: `year3`  
Duration: 60 days

Who this is for:

- The member has a real creative product, body of work, or project to build.
- The member needs rhythm, draft tolerance, and publishing integrity.
- The member can separate work from identity.

Who should wait:

- The member wants to become a creator mainly for validation.
- The member cannot yet keep a small work rhythm.
- The member wants to publish before they can stay with a draft.

Program promise:

Creation helps the member build one small real product through steady labor.

Modules:

1. `Sáng tạo như lao động`  
   Define creative work as repeated contact with material.  
   Output: creative labor definition.

2. `Chọn một sản phẩm nhỏ`  
   Select one product small enough to finish.  
   Output: product scope.

3. `Bản nháp và nhịp làm`  
   Create a draft rhythm and protect unfinished work.  
   Output: draft calendar.

4. `Chỉnh mà không phá giọng`  
   Revise for clarity without making the work lifeless.  
   Output: revision rules.

5. `Xuất bản không biểu diễn`  
   Publish as delivery, not self-performance.  
   Output: publish checklist.

6. `Khi không ai nhìn`  
   Continue when there is no applause.  
   Output: low-attention protocol.

7. `Kế hoạch 60 ngày`  
   Build, revise, package, publish.  
   Output: 60-day creation plan.

8. `Creator path nếu đủ điều kiện`  
   Decide whether to submit to creator/collaborator review.  
   Output: creator readiness note.

Workbook fields:

- product scope
- audience
- draft schedule
- revision rules
- publish checklist
- low-attention protocol
- creator readiness note

60-day calendar:

- Days 1-10: product scope and material gathering.
- Days 11-30: first full draft.
- Days 31-45: revision and structure.
- Days 46-60: publish or prepare submission.

Completion criteria:

- one product completed or submitted
- at least 20 work sessions logged
- revision rules used
- publish checklist complete

## Pro 06 — Wealth

Route: `/members/pro/wealth/`  
Tier: `year3`  
Duration: 90 days

Who this is for:

- The member wants to relate to money, investment, and future choice more consciously.
- Spending, saving, learning, and work decisions carry emotional charge.
- The member is ready to track honestly without shame.

Who should wait:

- The member wants financial advice, investment recommendations, or promises.
- The member is using the program to avoid urgent debt, tax, legal, or professional questions.
- The member is not willing to look at real numbers.

Program promise:

Wealth helps the member connect money to choices, attention, time, and future freedom. It does not provide financial advice or investment recommendations.

Modules:

1. `Tiền như lựa chọn`  
   Money is framed as stored choice, not personal worth.  
   Output: money story note.

2. `Chi tiêu để lấp hay để xây`  
   The member separates soothing spend from building spend.  
   Output: spending pattern audit.

3. `Đầu tư bản thân đúng`  
   The member applies the Right Investment filter.  
   Output: investment decision sheet.

4. `Lịch, năng lượng, tiền`  
   Every investment must fit calendar and energy.  
   Output: capacity check.

5. `Bớt đúng`  
   The member identifies what to reduce before adding more.  
   Output: subtraction list.

6. `Không mua danh tính mới`  
   The member notices purchases tied to imagined identity.  
   Output: identity purchase note.

7. `Kế hoạch 90 ngày`  
   Create a 90-day money-attention plan.  
   Output: 90-day plan.

8. `Tự do tương lai`  
   Define freedom in concrete choices, not vague success.  
   Output: future choice map.

Workbook fields:

- money story
- spending pattern
- building spend
- soothing spend
- investment filter
- capacity check
- subtraction list
- future choice map

90-day calendar:

- Days 1-15: observe money without changing.
- Days 16-30: categorize spending and investment.
- Days 31-60: reduce one leak and protect one building choice.
- Days 61-90: review future choice map.

Completion criteria:

- 30 days of money observation
- one spending pattern named
- one reduction held for 30 days
- one investment decision made or declined consciously
- future choice map complete

## Pro 07 — Family & Relationship Systems

Route status: required new route  
Suggested route: `/members/pro/family/`  
Tier: `year3`  
Duration: 60 days

Who this is for:

- The member sees family roles repeating in adult relationships.
- Boundaries, dialogue, resentment, obligation, and closeness need a new system.
- The member can work without blaming or idealizing the family.

Who should wait:

- The member wants to force another person to change.
- The member is not ready to look at their own role.
- The relationship context involves active danger or coercion requiring direct practical safety planning.

Program promise:

Family & Relationship Systems helps the member choose adult patterns inside close relationships. It does not guarantee reconciliation or another person's change.

Modules:

1. `Vai trò cũ trong quan hệ hiện tại`  
   Map the old role and where it appears now.  
   Output: role transfer map.

2. `Ranh giới mềm`  
   Build one clear boundary that protects contact.  
   Output: boundary script.

3. `Đối thoại không thuyết phục`  
   Practice conversation without trying to win.  
   Output: dialogue frame.

4. `Gần mà không gánh`  
   Separate love, responsibility, guilt, and over-carrying.  
   Output: responsibility map.

5. `Không đổ lỗi, không giả vờ`  
   Hold both truths: what happened mattered, and adult choice matters now.  
   Output: clear-seeing statement.

6. `Khi gia đình không đổi`  
   Build a plan for staying adult when the old system remains the same.  
   Output: unchanged-family protocol.

7. `Kế hoạch 60 ngày`  
   Apply one boundary, one dialogue rhythm, one responsibility shift.  
   Output: 60-day relationship plan.

8. `Tích hợp vào gia đình riêng`  
   Translate the work into the family or home the member is building now.  
   Output: integration note.

Workbook fields:

- old role
- current relationship pattern
- boundary script
- dialogue frame
- responsibility map
- unchanged-family protocol
- 60-day plan
- integration note

60-day calendar:

- Days 1-10: map role transfer.
- Days 11-25: practice one boundary.
- Days 26-40: practice one dialogue rhythm.
- Days 41-60: integrate into current family/home system.

Completion criteria:

- one role transfer named
- one boundary used

## Pro 08 — Children & Education Environment

Suggested route: `/members/pro/children/`  
Tier: `year3`  
Duration: 90 days

Who this is for:

- The member wants to build a healthier environment for children.
- The member is willing to start with adult reflexes, not only child behavior.

Who should wait:

- The member wants a script to control children.
- The member wants to outsource all difficulty to a method.
- The child needs specific medical, developmental, educational, or safety support beyond this program's scope.

Program promise:

Children & Education Environment helps adults build a home/learning environment with rhythm, boundary, attention, and real-life grounding. It does not replace professional care or individualized educational support.

Modules:

1. `Trẻ em không phải bản nâng cấp của người lớn`  
   The adult stops projecting unfinished ambition onto the child.

   Audit room, schedule, screen, adult mood, and expectations.  
   Output: child environment map.

3. `Ranh giới không bẻ gãy`  
   Build firm, non-humiliating boundaries.  
   Output: boundary language set.

4. `Màn hình, nhịp sống, chú ý`  
   Treat screen rhythm as environment.  
   Output: screen rhythm plan.

5. `Thành tích và cảm giác được yêu`  
   Separate achievement from belonging.  
   Output: love-condition audit.

6. `Người lớn chọn lại phản xạ`  
   Practice the adult pause before correction.  
   Output: adult reflex protocol.

7. `Kế hoạch 90 ngày`  
   Build one home rhythm and one learning rhythm.  
   Output: 90-day family environment plan.

8. `Nếp nhà cho thế hệ sau`  
   Define what should be passed forward.  
   Output: next-generation note.

Workbook fields:

- projection audit
- child environment map
- boundary language
- screen rhythm
- achievement/love audit
- adult reflex protocol
- home rhythm
- learning rhythm
- next-generation note

90-day calendar:

- Days 1-15: observe adult reflexes and child environment.
- Days 16-30: adjust one rhythm.
- Days 31-60: practice boundary language and screen rhythm.
- Days 61-90: stabilize home and learning rhythm.

Completion criteria:

- one adult reflex tracked and interrupted
- one boundary language set used
- one screen rhythm adjusted
- one real-life family rhythm held for 30 days
- next-generation note complete
