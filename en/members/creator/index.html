Internal Creator Program

From member to system co-creator

Creator access is opened only to qualified members after review. It stays inside the membership layer and is never used as public marketing copy.

Standard workflow

  1. Qualified member is invited into creator layer.
  2. Member reads guidelines and quality standards.
  3. Member submits writing, video, documents, or practical case.
  4. Admin review: approved / revision / reject.
  5. Approved content enters internal library or matching program.
  6. Contribution is tracked under internal operations policy.

Confidentiality rules

  • No public commission details.
  • No public payout terms.
  • No public recruitment copy for creator monetization.
