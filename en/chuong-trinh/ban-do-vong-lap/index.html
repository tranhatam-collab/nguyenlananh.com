The Loop Map Kit — 21 days

A toolkit with rails to take you from "I know I repeat this" to "I can actually choose again", in 21 days, through very small daily actions.

Register free to begin Read the base article

This is for you if

  • You keep repeating a reaction even though you've tried to change
  • You understand a lot about yourself yet "know it but still do it"
  • You want a concrete, doable frame instead of generic advice

What you receive

  • Loop map workbook (printable PDF) + root-fear question set
  • A set of 7 common loop cards for quick recognition
  • A 21-day calendar: observe → insert → replace, minimal cues
  • An insertion-point journal so the brain has data to relearn

The 21-day path

  • Days 1–7 · Observe. Each evening write one map line for a real situation.
  • Days 8–14 · Insert. Pick a prominent loop and place a small insertion point each time it triggers.
  • Days 15–21 · Replace. Practise the new reaction in real life and note the cost you paid less for.

How to take part

In the spirit of the system: you enter through a very light point first, with no early sales push.

  1. Register free with email and receive a magic link.
  2. Complete a basic profile so the system places you at the right starting point.
  3. Unlock the kit and the extended track in the members layer when you're ready.
