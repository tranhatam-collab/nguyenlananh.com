The Boundaries Kit — 14 days

Learn to say no without the guilt — moving from noticing when you are encroached on, to deciding what you need, to expressing it clearly and kindly, so you keep yourself while staying close to others.

Register free to begin Read the base article

This is for you if

  • You say yes then quietly burn out and resent it
  • You find it hard to refuse even tasks that are not yours
  • You want to hold a boundary without being tough or cutting ties

What you receive

  • A map of vulnerable zones + a deck of no-scripts (printable PDF)
  • A 14-day path: noticing → deciding → expressing
  • Sample kind no-lines for real situations
  • Accompaniment in the members layer for harder situations

The 14-day path

  • Days 1–4 · Notice. Log each moment you were encroached on but said yes.
  • Days 5–9 · Practise small. Say no to small, low-risk things.
  • Days 10–12 · One real boundary. Express one important situation clearly.
  • Days 13–14 · Look back. Which relationships changed, and in what direction.

How to take part

  1. Register free with Google.
  2. Complete a basic profile so the system places you at the right start.
  3. Unlock the 14-day path in the members layer.

If you are in a controlling or abusive relationship, setting boundaries alone may not be safe — seek trusted support. This program does not replace professional support.
