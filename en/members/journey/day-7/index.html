Journey Day 7

You are beginning to rebuild

You do not build a life through ideas. You build it through repeated visible actions.

Practice

Choose one small area: a desk, a room, or a corner you touch every day.

Reset it completely.

Rules

  • Do not rush
  • Do not do it just to finish
  • Do it with awareness

What you will see

You will see how rushed, messy, and avoidant you have been.

Continue

You are not only cleaning. You are rebuilding yourself.
