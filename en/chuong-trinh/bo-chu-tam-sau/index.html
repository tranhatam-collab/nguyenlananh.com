The Deep Focus Kit — 21 days

Recover attention worn down by the environment — through 21 days of rebuilding the conditions for focus, one layer at a time.

Register free to begin Read the base article

This is for you if

  • You work all day yet go deep on nothing
  • You open a task and minutes later reach for the phone
  • You want to win attention back without self-blame

What you receive

  • A session-setup card + an interruption-point map (printable PDF)
  • A 21-day path from base → extend → go deep
  • A daily journal to track the depth of each session
  • Accompaniment in the members layer to keep the focus habit

The 21-day path

  • Week 1 · Build the base. One uninterrupted 25-minute session a day, notifications off, a single task.
  • Week 2 · Extend. Gradually lengthen sessions; practise staying when bored instead of switching.
  • Week 3 · Go deep. One long session a day for the most important work, placed in a peak energy window.

How to take part

  1. Register free with email and receive a magic link.
  2. Complete a basic profile so the system places you at the right start.
  3. Unlock the kit and the focus track in the members layer.

If you suspect an attention issue that needs intervention, see a doctor and work with a professional. This program does not replace diagnosis or professional treatment.
