Family & Roots — 8 weeks

See your family system clearly, name what gets transmitted between you, and untangle generational loops safely — so you don't pass on to the next generation what you never chose.

Register free to begin Read the base article

This is for you if

  • You repeat your parents' reactions even when you don't want to
  • A few days home pulls you back into an old role automatically
  • You want to untangle without confrontation or cutting ties

What you receive

  • Three-generation mapping kit + boundary-practice cards (printable PDF)
  • An 8-week path: see → name → untangle gently → close the chain
  • Weekly boundary and role-exit practices
  • A family track in the members layer for harder zones

The 8-week path

  • Weeks 1–2 · See. Draw the three-generation map and find repeating patterns.
  • Weeks 3–4 · Name. Separate "mine" from "handed down", name what is transmitted.
  • Weeks 5–6 · Untangle gently. Practise soft boundaries and exit old roles in real life.
  • Weeks 7–8 · Close the chain. Choose the new thing so the old is not passed on.

How to take part

  1. Register free with email and receive a magic link.
  2. Complete a basic profile so the system places you at the right starting point.
  3. Unlock the path and the family track in the members layer.

If your family carries severe trauma or abuse, walk with a mental health professional. The program does not replace professional therapy.
