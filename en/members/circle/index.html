Quiet Practice Circle

A circle is not a community feed. It is an 8-20 person frame for rhythm, truthful check-ins, and real human review at selected points.

Email: - · Track: - · Reminder: -

Circle entry conditions

  • Track selected on the start page.
  • Reminder level and pause consent selected.
  • At least one honest check-in completed.
  • Acceptance that “I am avoiding” is data, not failure.

Size and rhythm

  • 8-20 people per circle.
  • One small practice each day.
  • One check-in each day.
  • One 30-minute weekly review.

No large feed

  • No likes, no leaderboard, no performance display.
  • Voluntary, brief sharing focused on avoidance points and real steps.
  • Private by default; public only when the practitioner chooses it.
  • The circle does not replace medical, psychological, legal, or crisis support.

Human touchpoints

  • Day 1: confirm track and reminder level.
  • Day 3: early avoidance review.
  • Day 7: weekly review.
  • Day 14: next-step recommendation.

Before the circle opens

The circle opens only after payment proof and pilot owner approval. For now, this page keeps operations aligned so the community layer does not become noisy.
