14-Day Pilot: small rhythm, real practice

This page prepares the 10-20 person pilot after payment proof. No large feed, no leaderboard, no promise of automatic change.

Email: - · Track: - · Reminder: -

Two pilot tracks

  • Gentle Rhythm: 5-10 minute steps for people who resist pressure.
  • Deep Facing: more direct questions, still held through small steps.

Valid check-ins

  • I did the practice.
  • I did one smaller step.
  • I am avoiding.
  • I need a real person to reflect back.

The 14-day rhythm

  1. Day 1: one honest line.
  2. Day 2: body and space.
  3. Day 3: first avoidance point, review people who choose avoiding or human reflection.
  4. Day 4: one object with emotional charge.
  5. Day 5: rhythm without force.
  6. Day 6: name one loop.
  7. Day 7: weekly review with “I keep avoiding...”
  8. Day 8: the next smaller step.
  9. Day 9: small creative labor.
  10. Day 10: one minute of sound-self practice.
  11. Day 11: one living-space corner.
  12. Day 12: what the ego does not want to admit.
  13. Day 13: a rhythm that can be kept.
  14. Day 14: avoidance point, real action, and next commitment.

Human touchpoints

  • Day 1: track confirmation.
  • Day 3: early avoidance review.
  • Day 7: short review.
  • Day 14: next-step recommendation.

Continue

Before the pilot opens, each participant needs a profile, a track, a reminder level, and at least one honest check-in.
