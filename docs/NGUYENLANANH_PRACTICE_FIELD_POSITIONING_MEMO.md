# NGUYENLANANH.COM PRACTICE FIELD POSITIONING MEMO

Date: 2026-05-03  
Status: Internal canonical memo. Hidden from public navigation and deploy surface until payment proof is complete.  
Audience: dev, content, legal, marketing, ops, Team Pay, Team 2.

## 1. Strategic Read

Nguyenlananh.com is not being repositioned because the current site is wrong. The current site has built the needed foundation: public trust, members routes, payment rails, email lane, admin, audit discipline, and bilingual surface.

The strategic update is about the product truth underneath that foundation.

The user problem is not only "I need good articles" or "I need reminders." The deeper problem is avoidance. People can read good material, receive daily reminders, and still stop when practice touches feeling, ego, shame, resistance, or the body. A stronger reminder does not solve that. A more beautiful article does not solve that. A noisier community does not solve that.

The site must therefore become a practice field: a place where people can notice avoidance without being shamed, take one small real action, and receive human companionship at the right moments.

## 2. Locked Positioning

Vietnamese:

> Một trường thực hành cho người sẵn sàng đi vào gốc rễ, nhận ra điểm né, và làm một bước thật với nhịp nhẹ, sự thật và người thật đi cùng.

English:

> A practice field for people ready to face the root, notice avoidance, and take one real step with gentle rhythm, truth, and human companionship.

## 3. What The Site Is

- A readiness filter.
- A guided practice system.
- A quiet rhythm holder.
- A bridge between written method and real human practice.
- A proof node for future VN and international platform work.

## 4. What The Site Is Not

- Not a self-help blog.
- Not an app for quick tricks.
- Not a mass-market course.
- Not a public social feed.
- Not a promise that change happens by itself.
- Not a replacement for medical, psychological, legal, financial, or crisis support.

## 5. Who The Site Filters In

- People who sense that reading more is no longer enough.
- People who can admit "I am avoiding" without needing to perform success.
- People who are willing to take a small real action.
- People who prefer quiet practice over public performance.
- People open to human reflection, small circles, and guided companionship.

## 6. Who The Site Does Not Chase

- People who only want motivational content.
- People who want immediate results without practice.
- People who want another intellectual layer to avoid feeling.
- People who want a noisy community or leaderboard.
- People who reject all inner facing.

This is not exclusion for ego. It is product clarity. The system must not dilute itself trying to convert everyone.

## 7. Two-Layer Product Model

### Fixed Core

The core stays stable:

1. Quan sát / Observe
2. Cảm nhận / Feel
3. Hành động / Act
4. Chuyển hóa / Transform

Primary fields remain:

- Dọn dẹp / living environment
- Cái chổi / object practice
- Lao động sáng tạo / creative labor
- Âm thanh tự thân / sound-self practice
- Nhịp điệu / rhythm
- Bản đồ vòng lặp / loop map

### Flexible Application

Application becomes human and case-based:

- small groups
- real cases
- mentor/creator formulas
- "đo ni đóng giày" tracks for people with similar patterns
- human reflection at important check-in moments

The fixed core prevents drift. The flexible layer prevents dead theory.

## 8. Two Entry Tracks

### Track A: Nhịp nhẹ / Gentle Rhythm

For people who easily avoid, resist reminders, or stop when a practice feels like pressure.

Rules:

- 5-10 minute steps.
- No guilt language.
- "I am avoiding" counts as a valid check-in.
- Reminder intensity must be chosen by the member.
- Human reflection is optional but visible.

### Track B: Đối diện sâu / Deep Facing

For people who are ready to meet emotional roots, ego reactions, and recurring loops more directly.

Rules:

- Still uses small steps.
- Prompts can be sharper.
- Human review is recommended at Day 3, Day 7, and Day 14.
- The track must not become spiritual pressure.

## 9. Check-In Policy

Valid check-in states:

- I did the practice.
- I did one smaller step.
- I am avoiding.
- I need human reflection.

Avoidance is not failure. Avoidance is data.

The system should treat a named avoidance point as progress because the product goal is truth first, not performance first.

## 10. Reminder Policy

Members choose reminder intensity:

- Level 0: no reminders.
- Level 1: gentle reminder.
- Level 2: rhythm reminder with one 5-minute step.

Every reminder must include:

- one small action
- a pause option
- no guilt
- no urgency manipulation
- no "you are falling behind" language

Required pause language:

- VI: `Tạm dừng nhắc 7 ngày`
- EN: `Pause reminders for 7 days`

## 11. Public Copy Direction

Public copy should become less academic and more action-first.

Every public article or pillar entry should eventually have:

- one insight
- one 5-10 minute step
- one short check-in prompt
- one soft CTA

Soft CTA example:

VI:

> Nếu bạn thấy mình đang né phần Cảm, hãy bắt đầu bằng một bước nhỏ trong nhịp nhẹ.

EN:

> If you notice yourself avoiding the feeling layer, begin with one small step in the gentle rhythm.

## 12. Community Direction

Do not build a large social feed in the next phase.

Build quiet circles:

- 8-20 people
- one practice per day
- one check-in per day
- one 30-minute weekly review
- human reflection for selected check-ins

The goal is not activity volume. The goal is retention through truth, rhythm, and human presence.

## 13. Pilot Before Scale

Before launching broad community features, run a 14-day pilot with 10-20 people.

Primary KPIs:

- Day 7 return rate
- Day 14 completion rate
- avoidance naming rate
- human reflection requests
- voluntary continuation rate

Secondary KPIs:

- payment conversion
- email open rate
- checkout completion
- article traffic

Traffic is not the proof. Honest practice is the proof.
