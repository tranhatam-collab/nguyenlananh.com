## 1. Voice And Product Rules

The paid system must keep the public voice:

- real-life language
- no spiritual performance
- no lecture tone
- no forced forgiveness
- no dramatic transformation promise
- no pressure-selling through fear
- no private case exposure
- every paid article must end in one concrete practice

The product rule:

```text
Public article opens recognition.
Core member lesson turns recognition into one practice.
Deep track gives repeated structure.
Pro program gives a 30/60/90-day implementation path.
Creator layer trains people to protect the system voice.
```

Stable public links only:

- `/phuong-phap/`
- `/hanh-trinh/`
- `/bai-viet/`
- `/join/`

## 8. Creator / Collaborator Curriculum

Creator layer is an internal quality path, not public affiliate noise.

### Roles

1. Content contributor
2. Editor
3. Translator / English meaning adapter
4. Practice designer
5. Web content operator
6. Visual assistant
7. Community moderator
8. Program coordinator

### Ten Creator Modules

1. `Creator 01 — Voice lock của NguyenLanAnh`  
   Output: rewrite a generic paragraph into NguyenLanAnh voice.

2. `Creator 02 — Viết không giảng, không chữa, không hứa`  
   Output: remove lecture, over-helping, and dramatic promise from one draft.

3. `Creator 03 — Biến một public pillar thành paid practice`  
   Output: one lesson + one practice from a public article.

4. `Creator 04 — Case thật: dùng được mà không xâm phạm riêng tư`  
   Output: anonymized case with no identifiable detail.

5. `Creator 05 — Internal links và route ổn định`  
   Output: link map using only built routes or planned route labels.

6. `Creator 06 — SEO/meta mà không phá giọng`  
   Output: title, description, excerpt for one member lesson.

7. `Creator 07 — EN meaning adaptation`  
   Output: English brief that preserves meaning without machine-like translation.

8. `Creator 08 — Worksheet và seven-day practice`  
   Output: worksheet fields + daily practice.

9. `Creator 09 — Submission packet chuẩn`  
   Output: complete packet ready for review.

10. `Creator 10 — Review, revision, approval, revenue ops`  
    Output: revision note and approval checklist.

### Submission Packet

Every submission must include:

- title
- intended route
- pillar/program mapped
- VI draft
- EN draft or EN brief
- practice block
- reflection prompts
- caution note if family, children, mental/emotional state, money, or health-adjacent topics appear
- stable internal links
- image brief and alt text
- ownership note

### Review Decision

Reject when:

- it sells through fear
- it promises a new identity overnight
- it exposes private cases
- it lectures instead of accompanying
- it uses route links that do not exist and are not labeled planned
- it has no practice

Approve when:

- voice is quiet, grounded, and specific
- practice is small enough to do
- boundaries are clear
- VI/EN intent is consistent
- the lesson maps to one pillar and one program
- the CTA matches the access tier
