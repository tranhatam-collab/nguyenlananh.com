Human reflection handoff

This page helps you move one true point into a human reflection handoff. You do not need to tell the whole story. You only need enough truth that the reader does not miss the point.

Email: - · Track: - · Reminder: -

Use this step when

  • The same avoidance point repeats for several days.
  • One honest line touches a root you do not want to keep facing alone.
  • You need a real person to reflect back so you do not fall out of rhythm.

Do not use this step to

  • Turn it into a long beautiful story.
  • Ask for an instant answer to your whole life.
  • Replace medical, psychological, legal, or crisis support.

The 3-line handoff

  1. Line 1: what I keep avoiding or repeating.
  2. Line 2: what smaller step I have already tried.
  3. Line 3: what exact point I want a real person to reflect back on.

Keep it short, concrete, and honest. The goal is readability and truth, not performance.

Draft the handoff here

If your latest check-in says “I am avoiding” or “I need a real person,” this section pulls momentum from your current local data so you do not start from zero.

This packet can be copied as-is and pasted into admin reflection when the handoff needs to move across machines.

Before you send

  • Return to today's check-in and make sure you chose the true state.
  • Keep one smaller step for the next day.
  • If the same point lasts for many days, move toward pilot or circle instead of staying alone.
