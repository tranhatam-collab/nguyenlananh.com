How Much Is Your Life Actually Worth?

A direct inquiry into life value beyond income, titles, and social recognition.

Main Insight

The worth of a life cannot be measured by income alone.

A life becomes valuable through contribution, depth, integrity, and the quality of presence you bring to others and to your own path.

When you redefine value clearly, your choices become cleaner and your life becomes more coherent.

Practice Step

Define your personal metric of life value in three criteria and review one weekly decision against those criteria.
