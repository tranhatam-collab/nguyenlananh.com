Deep Continuity is not a bigger reading library. It is the layer where a member repeats one practice long enough to see a pattern in real life. Each track has five lessons, one worksheet, one seven-day practice, one caution note, and one chooser.

---

## 1. Deep Track Standard

Every Deep track must answer:

1. What pattern is this track naming?
2. Where does it show up in ordinary life?
3. What practice lets the member observe it without forcing change?
4. What caution protects the member from going too far, too fast?
5. What next step is appropriate after seven days?

Default lesson rhythm:

- Lesson 1: locate the pattern.
- Lesson 2: see how it operates.
- Lesson 3: name the cost.
- Lesson 4: practice a small interruption.
- Lesson 5: review and choose next step.

Default CTA:

```text
Stay with this track for seven days before choosing another one. If the same pattern keeps appearing with strong charge, take it to the suggested Pro path only after you have evidence from practice.
```

## Deep 01 — Four Axes Audit

Route status: planned  
Suggested route: `/members/deep/bon-truc-tu-dinh-vi/`  
Source pillar: Bốn Trục  
Tier: `year2`

Track promise:

This track helps the member see which axis is underused or overstrained: observation, feeling, action, or Shift. It does not ask the member to optimize all four at once.

Lesson drafts:

1. `Tôi đang sống lệch ở trục nào`  
   The member maps one current life area across four axes. A common imbalance: too much observing without action, too much action without feeling, too much feeling without grounding, or using "Shift" as a fantasy of becoming someone else.  
   Practice: score one life area from 1-5 across all four axes.

2. `Quan sát không biến thành tự trách`  
   Observation becomes harmful when it turns into an internal accusation. The member learns to record facts without adding identity judgment.  
   Practice: rewrite three judgment sentences into observation sentences.

3. `Cảm nhận không biến thành chìm`  
   Feeling is information, not a command. The member learns to name sensations and emotions without building a story around them immediately.  
   Practice: name one body signal and one emotion, then stop.

4. `Hành động nhỏ có bằng chứng`  
   Action must be visible in life. The member selects one action so small it can be repeated even on an imperfect day.  
   Practice: choose and complete one action under 10 minutes.

5. `Review tuần: điều gì đã đổi chất`  
   Shift is reviewed through evidence: a pause before reacting, a clearer sentence, a smaller purchase, a cleaner corner, a choice not to rush.  
   Practice: list three small signs of different quality.

Worksheet: `4-Axis Audit`

Fields:

- Life area being audited
- Observation evidence
- Feeling evidence
- Action evidence
- Shift evidence
- Most neglected axis
- One seven-day practice

Seven-day practice:

- Day 1: score all four axes.
- Day 2: record one observation.
- Day 3: name one feeling.
- Day 4: do one action.
- Day 5: notice one old reaction.
- Day 6: repeat the action.
- Day 7: review one Shift sign.

Caution:

Do not use the four axes to grade yourself as a person. Use them to locate what needs attention this week.

Chooser:

- Reset Life if more than two axes are unstable.
- Inner if feeling or reaction dominates.
- Discipline if action is the weak axis.

## Deep 02 — Loop Map

Route status: existing  
Route: `/members/deep/ban-do-vong-lap/`  
Source pillar: Vòng Lặp  
Tier: `year2`

Track promise:

This track helps the member map one repeated loop without trying to destroy it immediately.

Lesson drafts:

1. `Nhìn vòng trước khi sửa vòng`  
   A loop cannot be changed while it remains invisible. The first work is not control; it is description.  
   Practice: describe one recent loop in four lines.

2. `Trigger không phải nguyên nhân duy nhất`  
   A trigger opens the door, but the loop usually carries old protection, learned belief, and repeated cost.  
   Practice: separate trigger from deeper belief.

3. `Phản ứng bảo vệ và cái giá`  
   Every old reaction once protected something. The member honors the protection while naming the cost.  
   Practice: write "This reaction protected me from..." and "Today it costs me..."

4. `Niềm tin đứng sau vòng`  
   Loops repeat because they keep confirming a belief. Naming the belief weakens its silent authority.  
   Practice: complete the sentence: `If I do not react this way, then...`

5. `Một hành động trước điểm khép`  
   The first change is inserted before the loop fully closes: one breath, one unsent message, one sentence, one pause.  
   Practice: choose one interruption point.

Worksheet: `Loop Map`

Fields:

- Situation
- Trigger
- Reaction
- Protection
- Cost
- Belief
- Interruption point

Seven-day practice:

- Track one loop each time it appears.
- Do not attempt to fix the first two appearances.
- On the third appearance, try one interruption point.
- Review what changed in body, words, and outcome.

Caution:

If the loop involves danger, abuse, coercion, or immediate safety risk, do not use self-practice as a substitute for practical protection and qualified support.

Chooser:

- Inner if the loop has strong emotional charge.
- Reset Life if the same loop appears across work, family, and money.

## Deep 03 — Silence Practice

Route status: planned  
Suggested route: `/members/deep/im-lang-that/`  
Source pillar: Im Lặng  
Tier: `year2`

Track promise:

This track helps the member build a real silence practice without romanticizing silence or using it to suppress emotion.

Lesson drafts:

1. `Tiếng ồn tôi dùng nhiều nhất`  
   The member distinguishes life-giving sound from avoidance noise. The question is not whether sound is good or bad; it is what the sound is covering.  
   Practice: list three repeated noise habits and the gap they cover.

2. `Cơ thể trong năm phút đầu`  
   The first minutes reveal the escape reflex. Restlessness is treated as data, not failure.  
   Practice: sit five minutes and mark when the body wants to leave.

3. `Điều xuất hiện khi không lấp`  
   Silence may reveal fatigue, sadness, anger, or nothing at all. All are allowed to appear without becoming a project.  
   Practice: write one sentence after silence.

4. `Im lặng với người gần`  
   Silence in relationship reveals whether closeness needs constant performance.  
   Practice: leave one small gap in conversation without filling it.

5. `Khoảng trống không cần biến thành nội dung`  
   The member learns not to turn every insight into a post, note, or explanation.  
   Practice: keep one silence insight private for 24 hours.

Worksheet: `Silence Log`

Fields:

- Time of practice
- Noise avoided
- Body signal
- Feeling or thought surfaced
- Escape impulse
- One honest sentence

Seven-day practice:

- Five minutes daily.
- Same time if possible.
- No phone, music, book, notes, or productivity goal.
- One sentence after each session.

Caution:

Do not use silence to punish yourself or withdraw from needed conversations. Silence is a return point, not a weapon.

Chooser:

- Inner if silence reveals repeated emotional material.
- Reset Life if the day has no true open space.

## Deep 04 — Self-Connection

Route status: existing  
Route: `/members/deep/am-thanh-tu-than/`  
Source pillar: Cô Đơn  
Tier: `year2`

Track promise:

This track helps the member notice where they leave themselves in order to be acceptable to others.

Lesson drafts:

1. `Tôi rời khỏi mình bằng cách nào`  
   Leaving yourself can look like pleasing, performing, over-explaining, joking, helping, or disappearing.  
   Practice: identify the most common self-leaving behavior.

2. `Vai trò xã hội và cảm giác trống`  
   A role may keep connection alive but block real contact. The member sees the cost of being only useful, pleasant, strong, or easy.  
   Practice: name one role and its cost.

3. `Nói thật với mình trước`  
   Honesty with others begins with a private sentence that does not perform.  
   Practice: write one uncensored sentence privately.

4. `Ở cùng người khác mà không bỏ mình`  
   The member practices staying present to their own body and limits during a real interaction.  
   Practice: check body once during conversation.

5. `Tạo nhịp gặp lại chính mình`  
   Self-connection becomes durable through small returns, not rare emotional breakthroughs.  
   Practice: choose one daily return cue.

Worksheet: `Self-Connection Audit`

Fields:

- Self-leaving behavior
- Role used with others
- Hidden feeling
- Private honest sentence
- Daily return cue
- One boundary or request

Seven-day practice:

- One private honest sentence each night.
- One body check in one interaction.
- One evening without filling the gap with screens or people.

Caution:

Do not use self-connection as an excuse to cut off all responsibility to others. The work is to stay with yourself while staying in real life.

Chooser:

- Inner if the self-leaving pattern is old and charged.
- Family if the pattern is strongest in close relationships.

## Deep 06 — Environment As Teacher

Route status: existing  
Route: `/members/deep/moi-truong-song-nhu-mot-than-the-thu-hai/`  
Source pillar: Môi Trường  
Tier: `year2`

Track promise:

This track helps the member stop treating environment as decoration and start seeing it as instruction.

Lesson drafts:

1. `Không gian đang dạy điều gì`  
   Rooms, screens, schedules, and people are always training attention and behavior.  
   Practice: name what one environment teaches.

2. `Nguồn vào số`  
   Digital input shapes desire and nervous rhythm. The member audits feeds, notifications, chats, and background media.  
   Practice: remove one input for seven days.

3. `Bàn làm việc và cơ thể`  
   A work surface can invite clarity or fragmentation.  
   Practice: set one work surface for one task.

4. `Người quanh mình như một môi trường`  
   People are not objects to optimize, but repeated relational climates affect choices.  
   Practice: note which interactions leave more clarity or more noise.

5. `Thiết kế một vi môi trường`  
   One cue, one boundary, one removal, one prepared object can change the path of the day.  
   Practice: build one micro-environment.

Worksheet: `Environment Audit`

Fields:

- Environment selected
- Behavior it encourages
- Behavior it blocks
- Input to remove
- Cue to add
- Boundary to set
- Evidence after seven days

Seven-day practice:

- Keep one environmental change stable.
- Do not add more changes midweek.
- Review energy, attention, and repeated behavior.

Caution:

Do not blame environment for every choice. The point is to reduce unnecessary friction, not remove all responsibility.

Chooser:

- Environment if the track opened a larger design need.
- Discipline if the environment is fine but rhythm is weak.

## Deep 07 — Gentle Discipline

Route status: existing  
Route: `/members/deep/ky-luat-nhe-va-nhip-song-moi/`  
Source pillars: Cái Chổi, Lao Động Sáng Tạo  
Tier: `year2`

Track promise:

This track helps the member build discipline without turning discipline into self-violence.

Lesson drafts:

1. `Kỷ luật không phải ép mình`  
   Discipline is a structure that helps action happen when mood changes. It is not punishment.  
   Practice: define one rhythm without moral language.

2. `Việc nhỏ và nhịp lặp`  
   A small repeated action creates evidence.  
   Practice: pick one action below 10 minutes.

3. `Ngày rơi khỏi nhịp`  
   The real discipline is the return after falling out, not the fantasy of never missing.  
   Practice: write a return plan.

4. `Nhịp có chỗ nghỉ`  
   A rhythm without rest becomes another form of pressure.  
   Practice: schedule one conscious rest block.

5. `Tạo bằng chứng bền`  
   Evidence is counted through returns, not perfect streaks.  
   Practice: review the week by returns made.

Worksheet: `Gentle Discipline Tracker`

Fields:

- One small action
- Fixed cue
- Minimum version
- Missed-day return plan
- Rest block
- Evidence count

Seven-day practice:

- Do the minimum version daily.
- If missed, return next day without compensating.
- Count returns, not perfection.

Caution:

If discipline language makes you harsher and less alive, reduce the practice until it becomes humane.

Chooser:

- Discipline if you need a larger rhythm plan.
- Creation if the rhythm is specifically for creative output.

## Deep 09 — Right Investment

Route status: planned  
Suggested route: `/members/deep/chon-dung-dau-tu-ban-than/`  
Source pillar: Đầu Tư Bản Thân  
Tier: `year2`

Track promise:

This track helps the member choose investments based on life capacity and evidence, not emotional urgency.

Lesson drafts:

1. `Mua cảm giác hay mua năng lực`  
   The member separates the relief of buying from the work of using.  
   Practice: name the feeling behind a purchase impulse.

2. `Điều đời sống đang cần`  
   The member identifies whether the real need is knowledge, practice, rest, structure, money, support, or subtraction.  
   Practice: choose the true need under the purchase.

3. `Chi phí tiền, thời gian, chú ý`  
   Every investment costs more than money.  
   Practice: calculate time and attention cost before purchase.

4. `Dấu hiệu chưa nên mua`  
   Urgency, comparison, exhaustion, shame, and fantasy identity are warning signs.  
   Practice: check the warning list.

5. `Bằng chứng sau bảy ngày`  
   A right investment produces small evidence in life.  
   Practice: define the first evidence before buying.

Worksheet: `Right Investment Filter`

Fields:

- Item or program considered
- Emotional state
- True need
- Money cost
- Time cost
- Attention cost
- Warning signs
- Evidence after seven days
- Buy / wait / no

Seven-day practice:

- Delay one non-essential purchase for seven days.
- Use the filter.
- If still aligned after seven days, decide calmly.

Caution:

Do not use this track to shame yourself for spending. The point is to choose consciously.

Chooser:

- Wealth if this reveals money patterns.
- Reset if buying is used to escape broad life disorder.

## 2. Deep Completion Criteria

A Deep track is complete when the member has:

- finished five lessons
- completed one worksheet
- practiced at least five of seven days
- written one caution note for themselves
- chosen the next step based on evidence, not urgency

Completion note:

```text
I do not need to collect more tracks. I need to stay with the one pattern that is already showing itself.
```
