The Energy Management Kit — 14 days

Stop spending energy like trees being cut — learn to move in your own charge-drain-release rhythm, through small daily actions.

Register free to begin Read the base article

This is for you if

  • You work all day without knowing where your energy went
  • You rest and still feel tired, as if rest doesn't pay back
  • You want to rebuild your rhythm from real feeling, not clock time

What you receive

  • A 4-battery energy map + tracking journal (printable PDF)
  • A 14-day path: noticing → rhythm → charge switch → adjust
  • Unlock prompts to turn drain into charge for each situation
  • Accompaniment in the members layer for hard-to-recover energy

The 14-day path

  • Days 1–3 · Notice. Log your energy feeling each hour, find the repeating pattern.
  • Days 4–7 · Set rhythm. Arrange the 4 batteries into your day by your real peaks and troughs.
  • Days 8–11 · Switch. Each day practise one charge switch from drain to charge.
  • Days 12–14 · Adjust. Look back at two weeks, fine-tune the frame for the week ahead.

How to take part

  1. Register free with Google.
  2. Complete a basic profile so the system places you at the right start.
  3. Unlock the toolkit and energy track in the members layer.

If you suspect a physical health issue draining your energy, see a doctor. This program supplements health care, it does not replace it.
