The Enough Workbook — 30 days

From a vague sense of lack to a clear baseline — so money goes back to being a tool instead of a master, through 30 days of writing the truth.

Register free to begin Read the base article

This is for you if

  • You earn more than last year and still feel not enough
  • You don't know what "enough" means for you
  • You want money to be a tool, not your only source of safety

What you receive

  • A baseline-of-enough template + a three-question card deck (printable PDF)
  • A 30-day path from looking → baseline → filtering → deciding
  • A weekly money-relationship journal to see the pattern
  • Accompaniment in the members layer for stubborn beliefs

The 30-day path

  • Week 1 · Look directly. Log the emotion each time you think about money, without judgement. Find the underlying belief.
  • Week 2 · Draw the baseline. Work out the "enough" number for your real needs.
  • Week 3 · Filter wants. Separate real needs from borrowed wants in one week of spending.
  • Week 4 · One enough decision. Choose one thing you will stop chasing, and one thing you will invest in for the long run.

How to take part

  1. Register free with Google.
  2. Complete a basic profile so the system places you at the right start.
  3. Unlock the workbook and the money-psychology track in the members layer.

This content shares a view on money psychology, not financial or investment advice. For important financial decisions, consult an appropriate professional.
