Creator Guidelines

Standards that protect the voice and quality of the membership system.

Go to submitBack to Creator Layer

Accepted submissions

  • Real implementation case or lived observation.
  • Clear structure: context -> action -> result -> lesson.
  • Grounded language: specific, honest, not performative.

Rejected submissions

  • Shock-copy written only to bait engagement.
  • Content exposing private third-party information.
  • Content conflicting with system ethics and philosophy.

Technical standards

  • Video includes short summary, timestamps, and learning target.
  • Documents include version, update date, and owner.
  • Articles include CTA back to practice/deep/dashboard.

Review outcomes

  • Approved: enters library and release schedule.
  • Revision: receives concrete feedback and deadline.
  • Reject: reason is logged to prevent repeat issues.
