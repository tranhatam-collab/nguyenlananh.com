Journey Day 2

Release the first loop

You do not need to do more. You need to interrupt one repeated pattern with one real opposite action.

Pick one loop

  • Procrastination
  • Phone scrolling
  • Avoiding hard work

Today's action

Do the opposite once.

For example: instead of avoiding, do 5 minutes. Instead of scrolling, turn it off for 10.

Rule

No perfection. One interruption is enough for today.

Record it

What did you feel? Where was the resistance? What appeared?
